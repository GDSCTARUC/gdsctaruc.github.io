About Us

Developer Student Clubs are univeristy based community groups for students interested in Google developer technologies ranging from topics such as mobile application and website developement, machine learning, and Google Cloud. Students from all undergraduate or graduate programs with an interest in growing as a developer are welcomed.

By joining DSC TARUC, students build thier professional and personal networks, also got access to Google developer resources. Students grow their knowledge in a peer-to-peer learning environment and build solutions for local businesses and their community.
